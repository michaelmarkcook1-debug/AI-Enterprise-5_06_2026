# Next Steps After Pack 02

After Claude Code completes Pack 02, do not immediately add more features.

## Step 1 — Review the App as a Product

Walk through:

1. Query
2. Understand
3. Assess
4. Demonstrate
5. Monitor
6. Investor Tools

Ask:

- Is the user journey obvious?
- Does every page answer its executive question?
- Are the strongest features visible quickly?
- Is the UI too crowded?
- Are seed/estimated labels obvious?
- Does Demonstrate feel board-ready?
