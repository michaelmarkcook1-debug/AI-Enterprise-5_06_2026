# Pack 04 — Board Pack Structure

## Objective

Define the board pack template.

## Board Defence Pack Sections

### 1. Cover Page

- Organisation
- Assessment name
- Date
- Prepared for
- Prepared by
- Confidentiality status

### 2. Executive Summary

- recommendation
- CIO Confidence Score
- Board Defence Score
- decision status
- top business benefits
- top risks
- required decision

### 3. Why Invest?

- business problem
- expected outcomes
- value drivers
- cost reduction / productivity / revenue assumptions

### 4. Why Now?

- cost of inaction
- market timing
- competitive pressure
- regulatory timing

### 5. Recommended AI Stack

- architecture summary
- vendor shortlist
- model/platform/app layers
- integration implications

### 6. Vendor Selection Rationale

For each vendor:

- role in stack
- reason selected
- evidence strength
- risks
- alternatives considered

### 7. Competitive Position

- peer adoption
- market maturity
- competitor use cases
- adoption gap

### 8. Risk Register

- risk
- severity
- likelihood
- mitigation
- owner
- status

### 9. Controls and Governance

- human oversight
- audit trails
- data controls
- DLP
- access controls
- red teaming
- fallback process

### 10. Strategic Sustainability

- future-readiness
- platform encroachment
- dependency risk
- switching cost
- reassessment horizon

### 11. Assumptions

- assumption
- why it matters
- failure trigger
- monitoring signal
- recommended response

### 12. Value Realisation

- KPIs
- baselines
- targets
- owners
- review cadence

### 13. Recommendation

- Proceed
- Proceed with Conditions
- Pilot First
- Reassess
- Do Not Proceed

### 14. Evidence Appendix

Every claim with supporting evidence.

### 15. Methodology Appendix

Scoring and confidence methodology.

## Acceptance Criteria

- Board pack template exists.
- Demonstrate can generate it.
- Evidence and methodology appendices included.
