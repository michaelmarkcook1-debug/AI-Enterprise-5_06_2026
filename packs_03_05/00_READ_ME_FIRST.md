## Core Principle

AI Enterprise cannot become a credible market intelligence or board defence product unless every score, claim, recommendation and export is traceable to evidence.

The system must distinguish clearly between:

- live data
- verified evidence
- documented public evidence
- vendor claims
- seed data
- estimates
- unknowns

## Non-Negotiable Rules

- Do not present seed data as verified.
- Do not publish claims without source traceability.
- Do not generate board packs without methodology and evidence appendix.
- Do not hide uncertainty.
- Do not hide stale data.
- Do not mix CIO buying advice with investor capital allocation logic.
- Do not remove existing features from Packs 01–02.

## Desired End State

AI Enterprise should become:

> A CIO and investor decision-intelligence platform that can discover the AI market, understand vendors, assess fit, defend decisions, monitor drift, and produce board-grade evidence packs.
